Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people to come and get the weather anytime, day or night. Maybe someone wants to plan what they're going to wear tomorrow depending on the temperature, or what they're going to do this weekend if it's going to be hot or not. If it's going to be raining for the next couple of days this would be the perfect time to do a little spring cleaning. This site will make a great source for someone wanting to plan their next few days around what the weather's going to be doing.

Target Audience:

The target audience for Casting-Fore is male and female, ages 35 to 64. An average economic income of over $25,000 per year. Primarily, mothers and gradmothers, secondarily people who enjoy the outdoors.

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Slab, serif (Bold)

I think this is a solid font with a subtle boldness that's easy to read on the screen and will make headings really stand out.

Paragraph Font: Raleway, sans-serif (Regular)

I think this font compliments the solid boldness of Roboto because the lettering is thinner and not bolded. It's also easy for me to read the wording in paragraphs.
